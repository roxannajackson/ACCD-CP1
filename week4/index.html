Roxanna's Week 4 Blender Homework

Below you will find the items I made for my introductory project to Blender. Prior to Blender I have utilized other 3D modeling softwares like Maya, 3DsMax, and ZBrush. So when our class first started learning it, a lot of the features, functions, and shortcuts felt familiar.

For this assignment I wanted to practice creating simple objects (like a glass bottle, pearls, gold bracelets, a wooden table, and a marble/stone tray) to get familiar with the tools that Blender has to offer. By creating these simple shapes it allowed me to play around with differet textures and surfaces to achieve a realistic look.

For the textures and environment I searched for several options on PolyHaven.


To achieve this look I searched for several Youtube tutorials to figure out how to make the glass bottle.


After making the most complex object, I then proceeded to make the wooden table, marble/plaster tray, pearls, and gold bracelets.


I found this assignment to be so helpful and quite fun to do! It has been several years since I dabbled in creating something in a 3D modeling software. I'm excited to see what else I can create!
