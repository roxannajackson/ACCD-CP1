Roxanna's Week 1 ASCII Art

This is the ASCII art I created:

One of my favorite shows of all time is Futurama. So I decided to make my ASCII art based on the Planet Express logo from the show.

When researching and looking to other ASCII art for inspiration I wanted to figure out ways to convey shadows, bolder lines, and negative space.

Kind of similar to how an artist would use a pencil to shade in certain areas to add depth.

I played around with a variety of characters—letters, numbers, symbols, etc.—and found myself gravitating towards symbols as my main medium.

Back when MySpace was all the rage (cicra mid-2000s), I recall creating something similar. So I found this assignment to be quite nostalgic.
